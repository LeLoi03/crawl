<p class="font_8 wixui-rich-text__text" style="line-height:1.38em; text-align:center; font-size:18px;"><span class="color_30 wixui-rich-text__text">This year, we experiment a new conference model. <p class="font_8 wixui-rich-text__text" style="line-height:1.38em; text-align:center; font-size:18px;"><span class="color_30 wixui-rich-text__text">All submissions must be anonymous. We use a somewhat relaxed implementation of double-blind peer review: you are free to disseminate your work through arXiv and other online repositories and give presentations on your work as usual. However, please make sure you do not mention your own name or affiliation in the submission, and please do not include obvious references in the text that reveal your identity. A reviewer who has not previously seen the paper should be able to read it without accidentally learning the identity of the authors. <p class="font_8 wixui-rich-text__text" style="line-height:1.38em; font-size:18px;"><span class="color_30 wixui-rich-text__text">A brief announcement submission must not exceed 5 pages and should not include any appendix.</span></p>

<p class="font_8 wixui-rich-text__text" style="line-height:1.38em; font-size:18px;"><span class="color_30 wixui-rich-text__text">Additional necessary details for an expert to verify the main claims of the submission may be included in a clearly marked appendix if extra space is needed.</span></p>

<p class="font_8 wixui-rich-text__text" style="line-height:1.38em; font-size:18px;"><span class="color_30 wixui-rich-text__text">Any submission deviating from these guidelines will be rejected without consideration of its merits.
