<p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">The </span><a href="https://www.araa.asn.au/conference/acra-2023/"><span class="xnormaltextrun">2023 Australasian Conference on Robotics and Automation (ACRA 2023) </span></a><span class="xnormaltextrun">is jointly organised by the Australian Robotics and Automation Association, UNSW, and the UTS Robotics Institute. The conference will be held in person on 4-6 December 2023 at UNSW Kensington Campus, Sydney.</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">Since 1999, ACRA has brought together&nbsp;Australian and New Zealand researchers and&nbsp;students in robotics, automation,&nbsp;and&nbsp;mechatronics. It provides&nbsp;a forum to present a&nbsp;breadth of research activities to the broader&nbsp;Australasian community. ACRA is a quality, peer-reviewed&nbsp;conference featured in Scopus,&nbsp;Google Scholar,&nbsp;and other ranking engines.&nbsp;</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">All full papers submitted&nbsp;to ACRA 2023&nbsp;will undergo a peer-review process. Each manuscript will be reviewed by at least two reviewers giving&nbsp;detailed comments. If the submission is accepted, the authors will submit&nbsp;a revised (“camera-ready”) version that takes into account&nbsp;this feedback. All papers are reviewed using a single-blind review process. Papers from all areas of robotics are encouraged.</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun"><strong>Organising Committee:</strong></span><span class="xeop"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></span></p><ul><li><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">Steering Committee: Prof Maurice Pagnucco&nbsp;(UNSW), A/Prof Yang class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p></li><li><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">Industry Chairs: Dr Marc Carmichael&nbsp;(UTS)</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p></li><li><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">Local Chairs: Dr Francisco Cruz&nbsp;(UNSW), Ms Gwendolyn Foo&nbsp;(UNSW)</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p></li><li><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun">Publicity Chairs: Dr Anthony Tompkins&nbsp;(UNSW), Dr Shadi&nbsp;Abpeikar&nbsp;(UNSW)</span><span class="xeop">&nbsp;</span></p></li></ul><p class="xparagraph"><span class="xnormaltextrun"><strong>Important Dates:</strong></span><span class="xeop"><strong>&nbsp;</strong></span></p><ul type="disc"><li class="xmsonormal"><span
