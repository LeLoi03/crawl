    <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-field-description field--type-text-with-summary field--label-above field__items">
    <div class="field__label">About</div>
      <div class="field__item"><p>The <a href="http://itcs-conf.org/">15th Innovations in Theoretical Computer Science (ITCS) conference</a> will take place at the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing on the University of California, Berkeley campus. It will be held&nbsp;<b>in person</b>&nbsp;from Tuesday, January 30 to Friday February 2, 2024. The conference will run in a single track and all sessions will take place at the Calvin Lab auditorium.</p>

<p>ITCS seeks to promote research that carries a strong conceptual message (e.g., introducing a new concept, model or understanding, opening a new line of inquiry within traditional or interdisciplinary areas; introducing new mathematical techniques and methodologies; new applications of known techniques). ITCS welcomes both conceptual and technical contributions whose contents will advance and inspire the greater theory community.</p>

<p>For participants requiring local accommodations, find our recommended list of options <a href="https://simons.berkeley.edu/accommodation-simons-institute-visitors">here</a>.</p>

<p><strong>Please see the ITCS website for the <a href="http://itcs-conf.org/itcs24/itcs24-program.html">event schedule</a>.</strong><br>
&nbsp;</p>
