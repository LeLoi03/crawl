 About INISTA 2023    
 Call For Papers    
 Prospective authors are invited to submit their papers to INISTA 2023. The topics of interest cover the entire spectrum of the multi-disciplinary fields of intelligent systems and related applications.  
 Call For Special Sessions    
 The International Conference on Innovations in Intelligent SysTems and Applications 2023 (INISTA 2023) welcomes and encourages the proposal of special sessions to be held during the conference.  
 Call for tutorials    
 INISTA 2023 invites proposals for the Tutorial Track. Each tutorial should cover a single topic in depth. Tutorials will provide an interactive space for research and experienced tutors to present the state-of-the-art research, latest development, and applications of specific intelligent systems.  
 Participation Modes    
 We are glad to announce that INISTA will be back to an in-person conference! We look forward to welcoming the participants to Tunisia. However, we will keep monitoring the situation of the pandemic to decide later if remote participation would be possible for those having travel restrictions.
