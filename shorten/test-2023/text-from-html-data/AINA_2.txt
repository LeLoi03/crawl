  Federal University of Juiz de Fora, Brazil, March 29 to March 31, 2023   
 The International Conference on Advanced Information Networking and Applications (AINA-2023) will be held at Federal University of Juiz de Fora, Brazil, March 29 to March 31, 2023. The conference covers theory, design and application of computer networks and distributed computing and information systems. The conference will include keynote addresses with speakers from both industry and academia. Prospective authors are invited to submit papers in any of the following areas (not limited to):  
 Communication Protocol and Architecture
