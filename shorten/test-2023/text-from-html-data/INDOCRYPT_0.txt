 Welcome to the home page of Indocrypt 2023, the 24th International Conference on Cryptology in India. The conference will take place during  10-13 December 2023   at  BITS Pilani Goa Campus  .  Indocrypt 2023 is part of the Indocrypt series  organized under the aegis of the Cryptology Research Society of India. Indocrypt conference targets researchers and academicians in areas, which include but are not limited to foundations, new primitives, cryptanalysis, provable security, cryptographic protocols, and (post)-quantum cryptography. Submissions on cryptographic aspects of network security, complexity theory, information theory, coding theory, and number theory will also be considered.
