 Welcome to IEEE SECON 2023!   
 SECON 2023 will be held in person, and we are looking forward to seeing you in Madrid!

IEEE SECON will offer full days of original paper presentations, workshops, keynotes, demonstrations, designed to further the in-depth understanding of the latest advancements in sensing, communications, and networking.
