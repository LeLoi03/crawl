 ICSOFT 2023 will be held in conjunction with DATA 2023    , SIMULTECH 2023    , ICSBT 2023    and SECRYPT 2023    .   
  Registration to ICSOFT allows free access to the DATA, SIMULTECH, ICSBT and SECRYPT conferences (as a non-speaker).  
  Although the conference is back to the normal mode (i.e., in-person) speakers are allowed to present remotely if unable to travel to the venue (hybrid support).    
 The purpose of the International Conference on Software Technologies, is to bring together researchers, engineers and practitioners interested on software technologies. The conference areas are "Software Engineering and Systems Development", "Software Systems and Applications" and "Foundational and Trigger Technologies".
