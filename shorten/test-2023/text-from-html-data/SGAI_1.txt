 AI-2023 : SGAI International Conference on Innovative Techniques and Applications of Artificial Intelligence  
  Cambridge, UK  
 AI-2023 offers a valuable opportunity to keep up to date with developments in AI and to share experiences in the practical issues of developing AI systems.  
 The scope of the conference comprises the whole range of AI technologies and application areas. AI-2023 reviews recent technical advances in AI technologies and shows how these advances have been applied to solve business problems.
