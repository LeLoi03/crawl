 Welcome to SAFEPROCESS 2022  
 The Organizing Committee has the pleasure of inviting you to participate in the 11th IFAC Symposium on Fault Detection, Supervision and Safety for Technical Processes, to take place in Pafos, Cyprus, 8-10 June 2022  under the auspices of IFAC, the International Federation of Automatic Control.   
 SAFEPROCESS 2022 will be held physically at the Aliathon Resort in Pafos, Cyprus. Physical participation is strongly encouraged, however to accommodate registrants who are unable to attend the Symposium physically due to the COVID pandemic, SAFEPROCESS 2022 will be held in hybrid mode, thereby allowing virtual participation.
