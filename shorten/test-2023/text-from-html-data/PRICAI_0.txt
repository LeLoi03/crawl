 Welcome to PRICAI 2023  
 The 20th PRICAI (2023) will be held in hybrid  mode in Jakarta, Indonesia. The Program Committee invites technical papers on substantial, original, and unpublished research in all aspects of Artificial Intelligence. PRICAI-2023 aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, educators and users in AI and related communities for in-depth intellectual exchanges, research cooperation and professional development.
