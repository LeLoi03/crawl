 23rd International Conference on Intelligent Systems Design and Applications (ISDA)  
 The International Conference on Intelligent Systems Design and Applications (ISDA) is a major annual international conference bringing together researchers, engineers, developers and practitioners from academia and industry working in all interdisciplinary areas of computational intelligence and system engineering to share their experience, and exchange and cross-fertilize their ideas. The first day will include tutorials and workshops organised by leading experts in the field.
