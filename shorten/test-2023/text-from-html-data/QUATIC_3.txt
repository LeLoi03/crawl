 QUATIC 2022   
 15th International Conference on the Quality of Information and Communications Technology   
 Talavera de la Reina, Spain | September 12-14, 2022   
 About QUATIC    
 QUATIC   is a forum for disseminating advan  c  ed methods, techniques and tools for supporting quality approaches to ICT engineering and management. Practitioners and researchers are encouraged to exchange ideas and approaches on how to adopt a quality culture in ICT process and product improvement and to provide practical studies in varying contexts. Information on previous editions can be found  here   .   
 About Talavera de la Reina    
 QUATIC 2022 is organized by the  University of Castilla-La Mancha   in Spain. The conference will be hosted in Talavera de la Reina, a beautiful and thriving city in the Toledo province, located near to the Tagus river. University of Castilla-La Mancha recently expanded the academic offer of Talavera with a new Computer Science degree.

 Software Quality journal - Special Issue    
 Best papers of QUATIC  will be invited to contribute extended versions to a  Special Issue  in the  Software Quality Journal   . The special issue is entitled  '  IT Quality Challenges in a Digital Society  '  .
