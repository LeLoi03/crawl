 How to download the Whova – Event & Conference App  
 The Whova event app  is free for conference attendees. To download the app, please follow the steps below:  

 Step 1  
 Search for Whova – Event & Conference App  on App Store or Play Store, or scan the QR code that corresponds to your device.  

 Step 2  
 Tap on Find My Event / Conference   

 Step 3  
 Search for ISWC 2023   

 Step 4  
 Sign in with the e-mail that you used to register to ISWC 2023.
