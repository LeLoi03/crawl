ICNP    
 IEEE International Conference on Network Protocols |  

 Since 1993, ICNP has established itself as one of the premier conferences in the computer networking field. ICNP deals with all aspects of communication protocols, from design and specification, to verification, testing, performance analysis, and implementation. Protocol functions of interest include network access, switching, routing, flow and congestion control, multimedia transport wireless and mobile networks, network security, web protocols and applications electronic commerce, network management, interoperability, internetworking home computing and networks and digital broadcasting.
