  The 16th International Conference on Distributed Computing in Sensor Systems  

  DCOSS 2020   June 15-17, ONLINE EVENT  
 Marina Del Rey, LA, California  
   
 May 25 - 27, 2020    
  
 DCOSS 2020 ONLINE EVENT: The online event will be available June 15-17 to registered attendees and will include the camera ready version of the papers, presentations of the papers as well as an asynchronous discussion forum with a separate discussion thread per paper. Detailed access information will follow by email to registered attendees soon.  
 Due to the health pandemic, DCOSS 2020 will be held June 15-17 in an online manner without physical gathering. Still, accepted papers (for the Main Event, Workshops and Posters) will be included in the IEEE Proceedings, as planned. Reduced registration fees apply. The online event will be available to registered attendees and will include at least the camera ready version of the papers, presentations of the papers as well as an asynchronous discussion forum. Detailed information will follow soon.  
 The annual International Conference on Distributed Computing in Sensor Systems (DCOSS 2020) will take place in Marina Del Rey, LA, California, USA  
 The conference is intended to cover several aspects of distributed computing in sensor systems such as high level abstractions and models, systematic design methodologies, signal and information processing, algorithms, analysis and applications.  
 For more information on the conference topics and the Call for Papers please click here  .
