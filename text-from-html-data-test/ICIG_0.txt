  Call For Papers   
   
 Papers in the main technical program must describe high-quality, original research. Prospective authors are invited to submit full-length papers in PDF format written in English at the submission website (https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICIG2023). Please follow the Springer’s Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) template (download link), with 10 to 12 pages’ technical content including figures and references. All full-length papers will go through a strict double-blind review process. Accepted papers for presentation at the conference will be included in the Proceedings of ICIG 2023, to be published by Springer’s LNCS series as well as be indexed by EI.The scopes of the conference include but are not limited to: o Color and Multispectral Processing o Biological and Medical Image Processing o Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition o Computational Imaging o Game and Animation o Surveillance and Remote Sensing o Multi-View and Stereoscopic Processing o Compression, Transmission, Retrieval o Computer Graphics and Visualization o Virtual Reality o Artificial Intelligence o Multimedia Security  Last updated by Dou Sun  in 2023-04-18
