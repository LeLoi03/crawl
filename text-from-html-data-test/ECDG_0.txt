 24th Annual International Conference on Digital Government Research      

 Portal Support Team    
  Published on: 24/01/2023  Event       
   18  to 22/06/2023     
   Tartu, Estonia   

 The Digital Government Society (DGS) will hold the 24th Annual International Conference on Digital Government Research – dg.o 2023 – in Tartu, Estonia, with a special focus on building safe and secure cross-border services.  
 The conference main organizer is the ERA Chair of e-governance and digital public services, Center for IT Impact Studies (CITIS) in the Johan Skytte Institute of Political Studies at the University of Tartu.  
 This year's topic is: Building Safe and Secure Cross-border Digital Public Services. Here is a video presenting the conference!
