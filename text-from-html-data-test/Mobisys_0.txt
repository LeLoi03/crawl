 Welcome to ACM MobiSys 2023  
 Arriving to Helsinki: please see our transportation page  .  
  
  The 21st ACM International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services. ACM MobiSys 2023 seeks to present innovative and significant research on the design, implementation, usage, and evaluation of mobile computing and wireless systems, applications, and services. This conference builds on the success of the previous twenty ACM MobiSys conferences. It is sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE. The conference will be held just before mid-summer which is a busy time in Helsinki.  
 Location: Helsinki, Finland
