Homepage 
 The IEEE European Test Symposium (ETS, previously ETW) is a well-recognized forum for presenting and discussing trends, emerging results, and hot topics in the area of electronic-based circuit and system testing. This annual event has undergone healthy growth and continuous improvement of quality since it was first organized in 1996.  
  This page provides links to past and future editions of ETS.

 The IEEE European Test Symposium (ETS, previously ETW) is a well-recognized forum for presenting and discussing trends, emerging results, and hot topics in the area of electronic-based circuit and system testing. This annual event has undergone healthy growth and continuous improvement of quality since it was first organized in 1996.
