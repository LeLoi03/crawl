 CPAIOR 2013    

 10th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 18-22, 2013, Yorktown Heights, NY.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 7874  .   

 This conference was preceded by a day of workshops and a Master Class on Computational Sustainability: Optimization and Policy-Making  .   

 Program Chairs: Carla P. Gomes and Meinolf Sellmann   
 Conference Chair: Ashish Sabharwal   
   
 Conference Website    

 CPAIOR 2012    

 9th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 28 - June 1, 2012, Nantes, France.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 7298  .   

 This conference was preceded by a day of workshops and Master Class on Scheduling  .   

 Program Chairs: Narendra Jussien, Nicolas Beldiceanu, and Eric Pinson   
 Conference Chairs: Narendra Jussien and Thierry Petit   

 CPAIOR 2011    

 8th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 23-27, 2011, Berlin, Germany.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 6697  .   

 This conference was be preceded by a day of workshops and a Master Class on Search Strategies  .   

 Program Chairs: Tobias Achterberg and J. Christopher Beck   
 Conference Chairs: Timo Berthold, Ambros M. Gleixner, Stefan Heinz, and Thorsten Koch   
   
 Conference Website    

 CPAIOR 2010    

 7th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, June 16-18, 2010, Bologna, Italy.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 6140  .   
 This conference was be preceded by day of workshops and a two-day Master Class on Experimental Evaluation of Algorithms and Benchmarking  .   

 Program Chairs: Andrea Lodi, Michela Milano, and Paolo Toth   
 Conference Chairs: Marco Gavanelli and Andrea Roli   

 CPAIOR 2009    

 6th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 29-31, 2009, Pittsburgh, PA.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 5547  .   

 This conference was preceded by a workshop and a two-day Tutorial on Constraint Programming and Integrated Methods  .   

 Program Chairs: John Hooker and Willem-Jan van Hoeve   
   
 Conference Website    

 CPAIOR 2008    

 5th International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 20-23, 2008, Paris, France.   

 Proceedings: Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science 5015  .   

 This conference was preceded by a day of workshops and a Master class on Modeling Practical Problems: The OR/CP interface  .

 Conference Website    

 CPAIOR 2007

 CPAIOR 2005    

 Second International Conference on Integration of AI and OR Techniques in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems, May 29 - June 1, 2005, Prague, Czech Republic.
