 Public Key Cryptography (PKC)  
 The International Conference on Practice and Theory in Public Key Cryptography (PKC) been sponsored by the IACR since 2003. PKC has been the main annual conference focusing on all aspects of public-key cryptography, attracting cutting-edge results from world-renowned scientists in the area. PKC oversight is provided by the PKC Steering Committee   
 PKC now has a Test of Time Award  .
