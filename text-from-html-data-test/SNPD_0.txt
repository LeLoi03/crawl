 SNPD 2023  
   25th IEEE/ACIS International Conference on Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Networking and Parallel/Distributed Computing (SNPD2023)  
 December 6-8, 2023  
 Taichung, Taiwan  

 Conference Venue  
 National Chin-Yi University of Technology, Taiwan  

 Important Dates:   
 Workshop/Special session proposal: June 16, 2023    
   
 Workshop/Special session acceptance notification: June 30, 2023    

 Submission Deadline:  September 4, 2023  Extended: September 25, 2023      
 Acceptance Notification: September 18, 2023  Extended: October 9, 2023    
 Registration/Camera Ready Paper: October 3, 2023   Extended: October 31, 2023    

 The 25th IEEE/ACIS International Conference on Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Networking and Parallel/Distributed Computing (SNPD 2023) brings together researchers, scientists, engineers, industry practitioners, and students to discuss, encourage and exchange new ideas, research results, and experiences on all aspects of computer and information science. SNPD2023 aims to facilitate cross-fertilizations among the key technology enabling areas and is soliciting papers in these areas.
