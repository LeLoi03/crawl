 IFORS 2023  
 Submissions are open!   
 Researchers, academics, practitioners, and students in any branch of operational research, mathematical modelling, data  
  analytics, or economic analysis are invited to submit abstracts or organize sessions.  

 Researchers who wish to organize a session or contribute  
  with an abstract within a cluster should contact one of the  
  chairs of the corresponding cluster.  
 The list of clusters and chairs is this one: link  .  
 Abstracts must be written in English and must have a | maximum of 2500 characters. 
  No participant can present more than one paper at the | conference. 
  The person submitting the abstract must be the one | presenting. 
    
 All accepted and presented abstracts will be published online as a monograph with ISBN / DOI identifier numbers.  

 IMPORTANT  
 The abstract submission deadline is  
  March 30th, 2023.   

 The submission process is handled through the system of EURO (the Association of European Operational Research  
  Societies). Submit your abstract at the following link:  
   
 Go to Abstract Submission    

 You must be registered at the EURO-online site to  
  access the submission system. Registration to the site  
  is easy, just go to:  
   
 https://www.euro-online.org/web/accounts/register/    
   
 If you are registered and forgot your login credentials,  
  you can recover them here:  
   
 https://www.euro-online.org/web/accounts/password-reset/
