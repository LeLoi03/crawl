 Welcome to 2025 10th International Conference on Signal and Image Processing!   
 ICSIP 2025丨Wuxi, China丨July 12-14, 2025   
 2025 10th International Conference on Signal and Image Processing (ICSIP 2025) will be held in Wuxi, China(中国 无锡)during July 12-14, 2025. It is co-sponsored by Southeast University, China, hosted by Wuxi Campus (Southeast University), co-hosted by School of Cyber Science and Engineering (Southeast University) and supported by Macao Polytechnic University (Macao, China), Technical University of Denmark (Denmark), University of Aberdeen(UK), University of Wollongong (Australia), Nantong Institute of Technology (China).   
  ICSIP is initiated in 2016. It was held successfully in Beijing (2016, hosted in North China of Technology), Singapore(2017, hosted in Nanyang Technological University), and Shenzhen(2018, hosted by Shenzhen Research Institute, Southeast University, China), Wuxi (2019, hosted in Southeast University in Wuxi), virtual conference due to COVID-19 (2020, hosted by School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University), Nanjing (2021, hosted by School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University), Suzhou (2022, hosted by Suzhou Campus, Southeast University), and Wuxi (2023, hosted by Southeast University in Wuxi), Nanjing (2024, hosted by School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Southeast University). As annual conference, it has attracted more than 1400 conference participants in last nine years. The aim of ICSIP 2025 will bring together the researchers, experts, and scholars from Asian Pacific nations, North America, Europe and around the world to exchange their research results and address open issues in signal and image processing. It is one of the leading international conferences for presenting novel and fundamental advances in the fields of signal and image processing.  
 2025年第10届信号与图像处理国际会议(ICSIP 2025)将于2025年7月12-14日在中国无锡召开, 会议旨在为与会者提供一个交流的平台，讨论信号与图像处理领域的最新研究成果和未来的研究方向。同时，我们希望本次会议能够建立起国家间，学术界与产业界，政府和非政府间的联系，能鼓励交流和分享，以促进信号与图像处理行业的发展。
