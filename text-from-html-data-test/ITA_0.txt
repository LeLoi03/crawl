 ICITA 2023  

 20 Oct 2023 - 21 Oct 2023  
 Turin, Italy   

 The 17th International Conference on Information Technology and Applications (ICITA 2023) will be held in a hybrid format from 20 until 21 October 2023 in Turin, Italy.  
 The event aims to bring together academics and industry professionals to share their knowledge on state-of-the-art research and development in information technology and applications. Additionally, the conference will help participants to establish new links and extend professional networks.  
 For more information regarding the topics of the conference, please visit the event page  .
