 International Conference on Technical Debt 2023  
   
 Welcome to the website of the ACM/IEEE International Conference on Technical Debt 2023.  
 TechDebt 2023 brings together leading software engineering researchers and practitioners to discuss approaches for managing various types of Technical Debt, to share experiences and best practices, and to identify key challenges for industry and academia.  
 TechDebt 2023 will be the 6th International Conference on Technical Debt and will be held in May 2023 in Melbourne, Australia. As in previous editions, TechDebt will be co-located with the 45th International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE 2023).
