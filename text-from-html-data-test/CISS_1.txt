 57th Annual Conference on Information Sciences and Systems    
   
 Submission deadline for 57th Annual Conference on Information Sciences and Systems (CISS) is approaching, December 10, 2022   

 The 57th Annual Conference on Information Sciences and Systems  (CISS) will be held March 22-24, 2023.  
 Authors are invited to submit previously unpublished papers describing theoretical advances, applications, and ideas in the fields of Information Sciences and Systems. Submission deadline is December 10, 2022. More information is given in the Call for Papers at  
 https://ciss.jhu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/CISS_callforpapers.pdf
