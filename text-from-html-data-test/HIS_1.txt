 The 23rd International Conference on Hybrid Intelligent Systems (HIS 2023) focuses on the combination of multiple approaches in developing intelligent systems. The conference aims to increase awareness, present cutting-edge research, discuss current trends, and establish collaborative opportunities. It will be a hybrid conference with in-person and virtual options.
