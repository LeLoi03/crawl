 WELCOME to the website of WiOpt 2023, the 21st International Symposium on Modeling and Optimization in Mobile, Ad hoc, and Wireless Networks. WiOpt 2023 will showcase state-of-the-art research contributions from different fields in networks. It welcomes original and high-quality submissions related to modeling, performance evaluation, and optimization of networks. The focus includes but is not limited to mobile networks, edge computing, intelligent networks, content-driven networks, cognitive networks, vehicular networks, robotic systems, Internet.  
 WiOpt 2023 will be hosted in Singapore, a modern island country known for its efficient policies and management in keeping up with advanced technology. This diamond-shaped island is the confluence of several major ethnic cultures in Asia, and simultaneously an international hub for residents and visitors from different corners of the world. The conference will take place at the NTU@one-north Executive Centre (can check the Venue  page for more details), which is near the central of Singapore, encompassing the city's beautiful neighbourhoods. Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) is the co-hosting university. Through its multi-disciplinary and research intensive curriculum, SUTD nurtures technically grounded leaders and innovators to serve societal needs.  
 WiOPT 2023 is grateful to the support of Key Laboratory of Cyber-Physical Fusion Intelligent Computing (South-Central Minzu University), State Ethnic Affairs Commission.
