 IFIP WG 7.3 Performance 2023  
 14-17 Nov 2023   
 Northwestern University - Chicago (United States)   
 https://performance2023.sciencesconf.org    

 The 41st International Symposium on Computer Performance, Modeling, Measurements and Evaluation will be held in Evanston, USA from 13th to 17th November 2021. The IFIP WG 7.3 Performance conference aims to bring together researchers interested in understanding and improving the performance of computing and communication systems by means of state-of-the-art quantitative models and solution techniques. The Performance conference has a long-standing tradition and has followed an 18-month cycle for many years. Recently, the interval has been changed to one year.   
 Scientific domain  : Computer Science - Networking and Telecommunication - Operating Systems - Mathematics - Probability
