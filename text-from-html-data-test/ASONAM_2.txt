 AboutComputing and Processing  
  Keywords:Social Computing,social network,,  
  Scope:The international conference series on Advances in Social Network Analysis and Mining (ASONAM 2022) provides an interdisciplinary venue that brings together researchers and practitioners from a broad variety of fields to promote collaborations and exchange of ideas and practices. ASONAM 2022 is intended to address important aspects with a specific focus on emerging trends and industry needs. The conference solicits empirical, experimental, methodological, and theoretical research reporting original and unpublished results on social network analysis and mining along with applications.
