 IVAPP is part of VISIGRAPP    , the 20th International Joint Conference on Computer Vision, Imaging and Computer Graphics Theory and Applications.  
  Registration to IVAPP allows free access to all other VISIGRAPP conferences.   
   
  VISIGRAPP 2025 will be held in conjunction with MODELSWARD 2025    and ROBOVIS 2025    .   
  Registration to VISIGRAPP allows free access to the MODELSWARD and ROBOVIS conferences (as a non-speaker).  

  Although the conference is back to the normal mode (i.e., in-person) speakers are allowed to present remotely if unable to travel to the venue (hybrid support).

 The International Conference on Information Visualization Theory and Applications aims at becoming a major point of contact between researchers, engineers and practitioners in Information Visualization, covering themes relating to Information and Data Visualization, as well as Visual Analytics or Scientific Visualization.

  2  .  Scientific Visualization
