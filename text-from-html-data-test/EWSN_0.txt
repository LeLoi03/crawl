 International Conference on Embedded Wireless Systems and Networks (EWSN)  

 The International Conference on Embedded Wireless Systems and Networks is a highly selective, annual forum for presenting research results in the field of networked embedded systems,broadly defined, including for example Wireless Sensor Networks, Internet of Things, or Cyber-Physical Systems. The forum has been established in 2004 as "European Workshop for Wireless Sensor Networks (EWSN)", was renamed to "European Conference on Wireless Sensor Networks (EWSN)" in 2007 to reflect the maturity of the event, and the scope was broadened to "International Conference on Embedded Wireless Systems and Networks (EWSN)" in 2016 to reflect the broad relevance of technologies covered by the event in multiple domains.
