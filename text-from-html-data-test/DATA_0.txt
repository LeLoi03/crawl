 Logistics:    

 DATA 2023 will be held in conjunction with ICSOFT 2023    , SIMULTECH 2023    , DeLTA 2023    , ICSBT 2023    and SECRYPT 2023    .   
  Registration to DATA allows free access to the ICSOFT, SIMULTECH, DeLTA, ICSBT and SECRYPT conferences (as a non-speaker).  

  Although the conference is back to the normal mode (i.e., in-person) speakers are allowed to present remotely if unable to travel to the venue (hybrid support).    

 The purpose of the International Conference on Data Science, Technology and Applications (DATA) is to bring together researchers, engineers and practitioners interested on databases, big data, data mining, data management, data security and other aspects of information systems and technology involving advanced applications of data.    
 Conference Areas  
 1  .  Business Analytics   
  
  2  .  Data Science   
  
  3  .  Data Management and Quality   
  
  4  .  Big Data
