  Welcome to the 22nd Portuguese Conference  
  on Artificial Intelligence  

  >>  
  05-08th September 2023  
  Faial Island, Azores  

 The EPIA Conference on Artificial Intelligence is a well-established European conference. The 22nd edition of the EPIA conference will take place at Faial Island, Azores in September 2023.  

 As in previous editions, this international conference is hosted with the patronage of the Portuguese Association for Artificial Intelligence (APPIA).  

 The purpose of the EPIA conference is to promote research in all areas of AI, covering both theoretical/foundational issues and applications, and the scientific exchange among researchers, engineers, and practitioners in related disciplines.  

 Proceedings are now available here  and here  !   

 For questions, contact epia2023@easychair.org  .
