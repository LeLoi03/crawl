 Monthly Featured   

 The biennial International Conference on Information Processing and Management of Uncertainty in Knowledge-Based Systems (IPMU)  aims to bring together scientists working on various methods for the management of uncertainty in intelligent systems. This conference provides a medium for the exchange of ideas between theoreticians and practitioners using different methods to address the important issue of uncertainty.
