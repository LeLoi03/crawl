 Global Wordnet Conference  
  
 The Global Wordnet Association is pleased to announce that the 11th International Global Wordnet Conference (GWC2021) will be held from 18 to 21 January 2021. Due to the international Covid-19 pandemic the conference will this year be held virtually.  
 The conference will be hosted by the South African Centre for Digital Language Resources (SADiLaR)  .  
 Keep your eye on the website for the latest updates on call for papers, venue, programme and other announcements.  
 Notification of acceptance: 13 November 2020

  Connection details to the virtual conference will be provided in January 2021 prior to the event.
