  Check International Conference on Internet and Web Applications and Services  happening in Valencia, Spain  on 06 - 10 Apr 2025   International Conference on Internet and Web Applications and Services covers the complementary aspects related to designing and deploying of applications based on IP&Web techniques and mechanisms. It comprises five complementary tracks. They focus on Web technologies, design and development of Web-based applications, and interactions of these applications with other types of systems. Management aspects related to these applications and challenges in specialized domains are aided too. Evaluation techniques and standard positions on different aspects are part of the expected agenda.
