  WAE Workshops  
 The proceedings of the first two WAE workshops appeared only as technical reports of the organizing institutions. The proceedings of more recent workshops appeared in the series Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS)  , Springer-Verlag  Heidelberg. For an overview  see the computer science bibliography  .  
 Since the year 2000 WAE is collocated with ESA. In 2001 it was decided to incorporate WAE into ESA, starting with ESA 2002.
