 Registration  

 Welcome to QCE23 Registration   
 IEEE Quantum Week 2023, the IEEE International Conference on Quantum Computing and Engineering (QCE23), will take place in the week of September 17-22, 2023 in Bellevue, Washington, USA.   

 QCE23 Open for Registration   
 QCE23 Registration opened on  World Quantum Day April 14, 2023.    
  QCE23 Early Registration ends August 14  Mon, August 21, 2023.   
  QCE23 Late Registration starts Aug 15  Tue, August 22, 2023.  QCE23 Registration Summary 
  QCE23 Registration Fees 
  QCE23 Important Author Registration 
  If you need registration assistance, please email [email protected]   .    

 Register  
  Now               

 With outstanding contributions and participation from the international quantum community, IEEE Quantum Week 2023 has formed an Exceptional Program   with exciting exhibits   featuring technologies from quantum companies, start-ups and research labs. QCE23, the fourth IEEE International Conference on Quantum Computing and Engineering, will feature a career fair   , 9 world-class keynote speakers   , 30 workforce-building tutorials   , 32 community-building workshops   , 153 stellar technical papers   13 stimulating panels   , 93 innovative posters   , and 4 Birds-of-a-Feather sessions   .   

 QCE23 Open for Registration   
 QCE23 Registration opened on  World Quantum Day April 14, 2023.  QCE23 Early Registration ends August 14  Mon, August 21, 2023.  QCE23 Late Registration starts Aug 15  Tue, August 22, 2023.  QCE23 Registration Summary 
  QCE23 Registration Fees 
  QCE23 Important Author Registration

 Before you register   
 With membership in IEEE or any of the IEEE societies sponsoring the QCE23, you will receive an approximate discount of 20%  on all available QCE23 conference registration packages and fees.

 If you are not a member — and before you register  — consider becoming a member by following the links below for information on IEEE and sponsoring society membership as well as their respective online membership application platforms. IEEE Member Application
