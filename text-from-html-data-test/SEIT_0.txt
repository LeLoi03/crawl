 The 2023 International Conference on Sustainable Energy Technologies (ICSET2023)  
   
  06/04/2023      

 The 2023 International Conference on Sustainable Energy Technologies (ICSET2023) is an excellent opportunity for experts, researchers, and developers to converge and discuss recent findings, new developments, and innovative ideas regarding energy and sustainable development. The primary objective of this conference is to provide a platform for scientists worldwide to share knowledge, establish international research collaborations, and expand their careers. Through this conference, researchers will not only share their new ideas and solutions to address energy and sustainable development-related issues but also identify new problems and research directions for future research and development.    
  
  Date and Venue:      
 Dates  :  November 10th – 11th, 2023     
 Venue  : Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam (IUH), 12 Nguyen Van Bao, Ward 4, Go Vap District, Ho Chi Minh City

  - Submitted abstracts must be genuine, concise and factual.
