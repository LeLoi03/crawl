 The ACM SIGMOD Anthology  

 The ACM SIGMOD Anthology is a digital library for the database systems research community developed by ACM SIGMOD with the cooperation of the VLDB Endowment, the IEEE Technical Committee on Data Engineering  , and the EDBT Endowment  , and with the current assistance of many publishers and individuals, both in providing permission to include material here, and in locating physical copies to scan into digital form. The bibliographic information in the Anthology is integrated into the DBLP Bibliography  . Michael Ley  is the founding editor and continuing inspiration for the Anthology. Michael created and maintains the outstanding DBLP Bibliography  . Curtis Dyreson  produced volume 6 of the Anthology. Many associate editors helped in preparing the Anthology volumes:

 About SIGMOD  
 The ACM Special Interest Group on Management of Data is concerned with the principles, techniques and applications of database management systems and data management technology. Our members include software developers, academic and industrial researchers, practitioners, users, and students. SIGMOD sponsors the annual SIGMOD/PODS conference, one of the most important and selective in the field.
