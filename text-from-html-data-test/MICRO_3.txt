  Extra Page Charges  
 Authors are allowed up to 13 body pages in the proceedings, excluding references and an artifact evaluation appendix (if accepted). You can purchase up to two (2) non-reference additional pages for CAD $210 per page during the registration process.  

  Extra Excursion Ticket  
 Attendees are able to purchase extra excursion tickets for $250 CAD until the early registration deadline on September 28, 2023 and for $300 CAD afterwards.  

  Refund Policy  
 Please note that at this time MICRO will no longer provide any refunds or registration cancellations due to visa denials or other reasons. If you have any extenuating circumstance please email the general chair  with an explanation for further consideration. This policy is simply a reflection of the fact that we have already made hotel food and beverage payments and other guarantees to various vendors. Thank you for your understanding.
