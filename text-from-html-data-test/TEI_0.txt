 Welcome to TEI 2023!   

 Welcome to The 2013 ACM International Conference on Tangible, Embedded and Embodied Interaction.  
 This year, TEI will take place in Warsaw Poland. TEI 2023 will be an in-person event with remote attendance possibilities.  
 We have an exiting programme for you including papers, pictorials, works in progress, arts, studios and student design competition.  
 We are looking forward to reuniting with the TEI community in Warsaw.  

 Register Here!      

 CONFERENCE INFO  

 26 February – 1 March 2023 in Warsaw, Poland  in University of Warsaw Library and Copernicus Science Centre
