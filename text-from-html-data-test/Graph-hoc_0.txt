 The Academy & Industry Research Collaboration Center (aircc) is organizing the International Conference on Applications of Graph Theory in Wireless Ad hoc Networks and Sensor Networks. The conference will cover topics such as connectivity analysis, data gathering algorithms, and secure routing protocols. It will be held at the Pullman Sydney Hyde Park in Sydney, Australia.
